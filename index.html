📖 How To Use Tether

Tether is a mindful cannabis consumption tracker. It is designed to help you pace yourself, reflect on your usage, and prevent automatic or excessive smoking habits.

1. The Core Loop

Complete your Pre-Flight Routine checks to unlock your daily session. Once started, take your hits and utilize the wait timer. Use the scratchpad to log your thoughts during the session. When finished, hit End Session & Log to recap your feelings and record the history permanently.

2. Day vs. Night Mode

Tether automatically transitions between modes at 6:00 PM. Day sessions have significantly longer mandatory wait times between hits and strict monthly limits to prevent daytime dependency. Limits for these can be modified in Settings.

3. Quick Hits ⚡

Need a tiny microdose? A Quick Hit bypasses your daily Pre-Flight routines entirely and doesn't count as a full day/night session limit. However, it enforces a strict Low Dose only policy, and has its own weekly limit. Warning: taking a Quick Hit during a Tolerance Break will bypass locks and reset your T-break progress entirely.

4. Locks & "Fuck It" Passes

When you reach your limits (weekly max, rest days, or manual breaks), the dashboard locks you out. You are given a limited number of "Fuck It" passes per month to override these hard locks and start a session anyway. However, bypassing a lock flags your calendar with a warning icon and resets your monthly automatic T-break tracker.

5. Emergency Grounding

If you ever get too high, green out, or feel anxious, hit the red 🛑 EMERGENCY STOP button in the sidebar. It provides immediate guided breathing visuals, a 5-4-3-2-1 sensory grounding exercise, and easy access to your emergency comfort links.

6. Clearance Tracking

The Clearance tab shows estimated clear times for saliva and urine tests based on your exact final hit time. These windows are purely estimates, but they dynamically shift depending on your tolerance setting and the THC% of the last pen you used.
